About Our School

Nurturing Confident & Responsible Students

Siddhardha Convent High School, located in Yadav Nagar, Malkajgiri, Hyderabad, has been providing quality education since 1984. The school follows the Telangana State Board curriculum and offers classes from Nursery to Class X.

Our mission is to nurture confident, disciplined, and responsible students who excel academically and socially.
