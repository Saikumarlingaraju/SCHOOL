Our History

A Legacy of Excellence

Siddhardha Convent High School was established in 1984 with the aim of providing quality education to students in Malkajgiri and surrounding areas. For over four decades, we have been a trusted name in education in the Yadav Nagar community.

Starting as a small institution, the school has grown to serve over 1,200 students across classes from Nursery to Class X, following the Telangana State Board curriculum. Our commitment to academic excellence, moral values, and holistic development has remained unwavering throughout our journey.
