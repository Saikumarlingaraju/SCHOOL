Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the admission age for Nursery?

Children who are 3 years old by June of the academic year are eligible for admission to Nursery.

What board does the school follow?

We follow the Telangana State Board (BSE Telangana) curriculum for all classes from Nursery to Class X.

Is transport facility available?

Yes, we provide safe school bus transport covering multiple routes in and around Malkajgiri and Secunderabad. Transport fees are charged separately.

What medium of instruction is offered?

We offer both English Medium and Telugu Medium instruction to cater to different student needs.

When do admissions open?

Admissions typically open in March for the upcoming academic year. Contact the school office for specific dates and availability of seats.
